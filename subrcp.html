Rajma Chawal

Kidney beans curry served with steamed rice

Prep: 20 mins
|
Cook: 1 hr
|
Serves: 4

For Chawal (Rice):

Cooking Directions

1

Prepare the Rajma

Drain the soaked kidney beans and pressure cook with 4 cups of water and salt for about 15-20 minutes until they are soft and tender.

2

Prepare the Base

Heat oil or ghee in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.

3

Add Aromatics

Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies. Sauté for 2 minutes until raw smell disappears.

4

Add Spices

Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, and rajma masala. Mix well and cook for a minute.

5

Add Tomatoes

Pour in the tomato puree and cook until oil separates from the masala, about 8-10 minutes.

6

Combine and Simmer

Add the cooked rajma along with its water. Mix well and simmer for 15-20 minutes until the gravy thickens.

7

Prepare Rice

Wash basmati rice and soak for 15 minutes. Drain and cook with water, ghee/oil, and salt until fluffy.

8

Garnish and Serve

Garnish rajma with fresh coriander. Serve hot with steamed rice and lemon wedges.
